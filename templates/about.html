About the Fastest Food Finder

Fastest Food Finder is a service for you to log and view the times you order food for delivery. By providing your location, the restaurant and the times the order was placed and arrived, we can provide recommendations to get you the fastest meal possible at any given time

This is an open-source project for CS 411 - Database Systems!
